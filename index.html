About Me

 Haoran Yang is currently a Ph.D. candidate at Data Science and Machine Intelligence Lab of Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology, University of Technology Sydney, supervised by Prof. Guandong Xu and Dr. Hongxu Chen. He also works closely with Dr. Xiangyu Zhao at City University of Hong Kong. Prior to that, he obtained his B.Sc. in computer science and technology and B.Eng Minor in financial engineering from Nanjing University in 2020, supervised by Prof. Chongjun Wang. During Haoran's undergraduate study, he was a visiting student of IFM Lab at Florida State University from 07/2019 to 10/2019, supervised by Dr. Jiawei Zhang.

Haoran's research interests include but not limited to Graph Neural Networks and Recommendation Systems. Currently, he focuses on the following research topics:
  • Graph Contrastive Learning (GCL)
  • GCL's Application in Recommendation Systems
